Features

Infinite Creative Superpowers

Aura AI handles the heavy lifting of geometric modeling, UV unwrapping, and material creation so you can focus on building experiences.

AI Prompt-to-Mesh

Type any description of a 3D asset or landscape and watch Aura generate watertight meshes with optimized polycounts in real-time.

Smart Material Node

Automatically applies high-resolution PBR textures, standard roughness maps, normals, and ambient occlusion parameters to match your style.

Multi-Engine Export

Seamlessly export your generated worlds into Unreal Engine, Unity, Blender, or WebGL with pre-configured materials ready to go.

Designed for Next-Gen Spatial Computing

Whether you're developing high-fidelity games, immersive VR environments, or mixed reality applications for spatial headsets, Aura AI provides responsive, scalable tools.

Optimized Topology

Meshes are created with clean quad structures, making them easy to edit and modify inside modeling packages.

Consistent Global Illumination

Optionally bake lighting configurations and ambient shadows directly into texture maps for immediate setup.
